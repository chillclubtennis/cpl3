Team Roster Rules
  • Minimum team size: 9 players
  • Maximum team size: Unlimited
  • At least 4 players must have UTR ≤ 4.25 (emerging players)
  • At least 3 players must have UTR ≥ 4.5 (top players)
Lines on Match Day
  • 4 Singles: S1, S2, S1R, S2R
  • 4 Doubles: D1, D2, D1R, D2R
  • First Half: S1, S2, D1, D2
  • Second Half: S1R, S2R, D1R, D2R
Rules
  • A player can play in both halves, but cannot face the same opponent twice.
  • Doubles pairs must stay together (no re-pairing).
  • Captains may also use fresh players in the second half.
  • Same player can't play Singles and Doubles on the same day.
Team Size per Match
  • Minimum: 6 players
  • Maximum: 12 players
Emerging Player Rules[Round Robin]
Emerging Player (EP) Rule
  • At least 2 of the 8 lines must be EP lines.
  • At least 2 players must be EP
  • If no EP is available, that line is a bye.
  • Captains are free to play more than 2 EPs if they want.
  • Captains should give EPs genuine opportunities, not just meet the bare minimum.

What Counts as an EP Line?
  • Singles line played by an EP
  • Doubles line: EP + Star
  • Doubles line: EP + EP (still only one EP line)
Emerging Player Rules[Playoffs]
Emerging Player (EP) Rule
  • Two dedicated lines for EP. 1 Singles S5 and 1 Doubles D5
  • If no EP is available, that line is a bye.
  • Round robin EP rules don't apply for playoffs
What Counts as an EP Line?
  • Singles line played by an EP
  • Doubles line: EP + EP
EP Player Criteria
  • Only singles rating is considered for EP status (doubles rating is ignored).
  • If no rating at all, player can still be EP (same rule for everyone).
  • If no singles rating but doubles rating exists, doubles rating will be used.
  • Reliability percentage does not matter. Whatever rating exists will be considered.
