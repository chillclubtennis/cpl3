üèÜ Playoff Format

The playoff format for CPL 3.0 is as follows:

  • P1: Top 3 vs Top 6 and Top 4 vs Top 5
  • P2: Winner of Top 3/6 vs Top 2 and Winner of Top 4/5 vs Top 1
  • Final: Winner of the two P2 matches

Top 1 and Top 2 teams get a bye week and play in the second round.
