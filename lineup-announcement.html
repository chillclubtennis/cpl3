Late Arrival Rules

  • First-half players: Must be on court at match start (arrive 15 min early).
  • Second-half players: Must be on court 45 min after match start (arrive 15 min early).
  • If not present at start time: Lose toss privilege, opponent decides.

Late penalties (from scheduled start time):

  • 5 min late: Forfeit 1 game
  • 10 min late: Forfeit 2 games
  • 15 min late: Forfeit 3 games
  • 20+ min late: Match line forfeited

4Ô∏è‚É£ Combination Rules

  • Simple rule to remember: In the 2nd half, if the same player plays, they shouldn't play the same opponent.
  • No mixing singles and doubles players.
  • No splitting of doubles pairs. In the second half, if you want different players, both should be different.
