📢 Lineup Announcement Rules – CPL 3.0

1️⃣ When to Announce

Captains must announce the full lineup (both halves) before the match starts.

Keeps things transparent & avoids mid-match reshuffling. ✅

2️⃣ Player Reporting

  • First half players: must be present at match start.
  • Second half players: must report within 45 mins of start time.
  • Standard late penalties apply ⏱️

3️⃣ Format to Announce (examples)

Example 1:

S1 – Name1  
S2 – Name2  
D1 – Name3 / Name4 (EP)  
D2 – Name5 / Name6  
S3 – Name7  
S4 – Name8  
D3 – Name9 / Name10  
D4 – Name11 / Name12 (EP)

Example 2:

S1 / S3 – Name1  
S2 – Name2  
D1 / D3 – Name3 / Name4 (EP)  
D2 / D4 – Name5 / Name6  
S4 – Name7 (EP)

4️⃣ Same Player Playing Reverse Rules

  • If S1 plays reverse too as S3, he will play S3of the oppoenent.
  • If S2 plays reverse too as S4, he will play S4of the oppoenent.
  • Same applies to D1, D2 while playing reverse
  • If both teams are playing the same players in 2nd half. They just exchange opponents as we did in season2
  • Simple rule to remember : 2 nd half if same player plays he shouldn't play the same oppoenet
  • No mixing singles and doubles players
  • No splitting of doubles pairs. Second half you want different players, both should be different
